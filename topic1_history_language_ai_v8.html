The History of Language AI

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is the field devoted to building systems that can understand, learn, and make decisions in ways that resemble human intelligence.

What is Language AI?

Language AI helps computers work with human language — reading, writing, translating, and conversing in useful ways.

💬
ChatGPT launched publicly (Nov 2022)
Reached ~1 million users in 5 days — unprecedented speed.
📈
Language AI moved from labs to everyday use.

How Language AI Evolved

🧺

Bag‑of‑Words — Counting

Represent a sentence by counting word frequencies; order and meaning are ignored.

🗺️

word2vec (2013) — Meaning

Turn words into number lists (vectors) so that words used in similar ways sit near each other.

🔦

Attention (2017) — Context

A focus mechanism: the model highlights the most relevant words when understanding or generating text. Paper: “Attention Is All You Need” (2017)
